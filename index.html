Projects

Crit Moves Conversational Tool

Crit Moves Conversation Tool is a tool created by me and my Capstone group for professors in the Tyler School of Art and Architecture who want students more engaged and interactive during their studio classes. The Crit Moves Conversational Tool will be an application which can be accessed through any device and it will allow the students at Tyler School of Arts to engage in more interactive discussions.

TUDash

TUDash is an application that is still in the design phase. It is an application that is designed to consolidate all the food options at Temple University on one application with the feature that allows you to pick up at your convenience. Any food truck, any food stall from all over Temple University, Main Campus at the touch of your fingertips. You pick the time and place, and the food is gauranteed to be ready!
